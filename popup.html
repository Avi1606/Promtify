📖 Setup & Usage Guide
  1. Get a free Gemini key from Google AI Studio.
  2. Paste it into the API key field above and click Save.
  3. Select your preferred model (e.g., Gemini 2.5 Flash).
  4. Go to ChatGPT and start typing a prompt.
  5. Click the green ✨ Optimize button next to the Send button.
  6. Your prompt will be rewritten for clarity and token efficiency automatically!
